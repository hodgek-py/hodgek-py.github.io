A Little About Me
I grew up on a farm around Drumheller and went to Morrin School from elementary to the end of high school. In 2021 I graduated from the University of Lethbridge with a Bachelor of Science in Statistics and a concentration in Economics. Even from a young age I have always had a fascination with computers and video games, spending many hours building computers and also enjoying hours of video games.
Aspirations and Coding Knowledge
It was not until I graduated from university that I fully took an interest in coding and website making. Through university I had used some coding languages like R and C#, although to a limited capacity. At the moment I currently know Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and MySQL. In my spare time I design websites and develop my data analysis. My newest project is a website that can run statical analysis using excel and csv files. The backend code uses the Python flask framework and HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery for the frontend.
